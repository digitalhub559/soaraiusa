Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get my free scorecard? +

We aim to deliver your free 2-page scorecard within 48 hours of submission. Most reports are completed within 24 hours. You'll receive an email with a download link and summary of your top 3 improvement opportunities.

What makes the Pro report worth $399? +

The Pro report includes 10 headline variations, 6 different CTA designs, complete page rewrite suggestions, 5 A/B test templates with sample size calculations, objection-handling copy, a complete implementation roadmap, and competitive analysis. Most customers see ROI within their first A/B test (typically 2-4 weeks).

How does the Ad Copy Generator work? +

Submit your product details, target audience, and preferred platforms through our intake form. Our AI analyzes your inputs and generates 5-8 unique ad variations per platform using proven frameworks (AIDA, PAS, BAB, 4Ps). You get copy-paste ready ads with scoring, rationale, and A/B testing recommendations.

Which ad platforms are supported? +

We generate optimized copy for Google Ads, Meta (Facebook/Instagram), LinkedIn, and TikTok. Each platform's copy respects character limits, best practices, and native formatting requirements.

Can I upgrade from Free to Pro? +

Absolutely! Many customers start with the free scorecard, then upgrade to Pro. If you upgrade within 30 days of receiving your free scorecard, we'll credit it towards your upgrade and provide continuity on your analysis.

What if I'm not satisfied with the results? +

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on the Pro report. If you're not satisfied with the recommendations and analysis, contact us within 30 days for a full refund. No questions asked.

What does the SEO Audit check? +

Our SEO audit checks over 50 factors including page speed, mobile readiness, SSL security, meta tags, heading structure, image optimization, structured data, internal linking, content depth, and AI-powered content quality assessment with E-E-A-T scoring.

How often does the Pro SEO Audit re-crawl my site? +

Pro subscribers get weekly automated re-crawls of their website. You'll receive email alerts if any SEO regressions are detected, such as broken links, speed drops, or missing meta tags. Monthly PDF reports track your progress over time.
