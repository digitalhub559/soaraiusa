Why Regular SEO Audits Matter

Search engines update their algorithms hundreds of times each year. What worked last quarter may be penalizing you today.

A comprehensive SEO audit is not a one-time task — it is an ongoing practice that separates businesses with growing organic traffic from those watching their rankings decline. Technical debt accumulates silently: a developer pushes a noindex tag to production, a CMS update breaks your structured data, or a new page creates a redirect chain that dilutes link equity. Without regular audits, these issues compound until you notice a traffic drop — often months after the damage began.

Our AI-powered audit catches problems the moment they appear. It evaluates over 150 ranking factors across technical health, content quality, backlink profile, and the emerging field of Generative Engine Optimization. GEO readiness is particularly critical in 2026 as AI assistants like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews increasingly answer queries by citing web pages directly — pages that are structured for machine readability rank higher in these new channels.

Is this SEO audit really free?

Yes. The standard audit is completely free with no login, no credit card, and no usage limits. We also offer a Pro audit with deeper analysis and implementation support for businesses that need hands-on help.

What is GEO readiness and why does it matter?

GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) measures how well your content is structured for AI-powered search engines. As tools like Google AI Overviews and ChatGPT increasingly cite web sources, GEO-ready pages get more visibility in these new channels.

How long does the audit take?

Most audits complete in under two minutes. Larger sites with hundreds of pages may take slightly longer as our crawler evaluates more URLs.
