What's the difference between keyword difficulty and search intent? +
Keyword difficulty (0-100) tells you how hard it is to rank for a keyword based on competition. Search intent tells you what the user is looking for: Informational (learning), Commercial (research before purchase), Transactional (ready to buy), or Navigational (finding a specific site). A high-difficulty keyword might still be worth targeting if it has strong commercial intent and aligns with your business goals.

Create high-quality content around these keywords, optimize your meta tags and headings, build backlinks, and monitor your rankings monthly.
