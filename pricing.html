FAQ

Common Questions

Are the free tools really free forever?
Yes. Our four AI tools (SEO Audit, Keyword Research, LPO Scorecard, Ad Copy Generator) are permanently free with no usage limits, no signup, and no credit card required. Use them on any website, as many times as you want.
How is SoarAI so much cheaper than a traditional agency?
AI does the heavy lifting. Our AI systems handle 80% of the analysis, auditing, and content generation that agencies charge hundreds of hours for. Our experts review, refine, and implement — so you get agency-quality output at a fraction of the cost and timeline.
What's the difference between Pro and Full-Service Agency?
Pro services ($299–$499) are focused engagements — one deliverable, one problem solved. Full-Service Agency ($2.5K–$10K+) covers larger-scope projects like building hundreds of SEO pages, redesigning your entire site, or launching multi-channel ad campaigns. Most clients start with one Pro service, then go Full-Service once they see results.
Do I own the deliverables?
100%. All code, content, pages, campaigns, and reports are yours to keep. Full ownership — no recurring fees, no licensing, no lock-in.
How long do projects take?
Pro services: 2–3 weeks. Programmatic SEO: 2–4 weeks. Website Redesign: 3–5 weeks. Competitor Intelligence: 1–2 weeks. We provide a specific timeline during the scoping call.
Can I combine Pro with a Full-Service project?
Absolutely — and most clients do. A typical combination is SEO Pro (to fix your existing site) plus Programmatic SEO (to expand your footprint). We offer a 15% discount when you bundle two or more services.
Do you integrate with my existing tools?
Yes. We work with Google Analytics, Search Console, Google Ads, Meta Ads Manager, HubSpot, Salesforce, Mailchimp, Brevo, WhatsApp Business, and most major CRMs and marketing platforms. No rip-and-replace required.
What if I'm not satisfied?
We offer a satisfaction guarantee on all Pro services. If the deliverables don't meet the scope we agreed on, we'll revise them at no additional cost. Our 30-day post-delivery support ensures you're never left hanging.
Do you work with agencies and resellers?
Yes. We offer white-label and partnership programs for agencies, consultants, and technology platforms looking to add SEO, content, and ads capabilities. Reach out to discuss partnership terms.
