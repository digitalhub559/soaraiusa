Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use SoarAI, we collect the following information:

Account Information: Name and email address when you sign in with Google.

Service Data: Website URLs you submit for analysis, ad copy inputs (product details, target audience, platform preferences), and SEO audit requests.

Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, and interaction data collected via Google Analytics (anonymized).

Payment Information: Payment details are processed securely by our payment provider (Stripe). We do not store credit card numbers on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to deliver our services (landing page scorecards, ad copy generation, SEO audits), process payments, send service-related communications, and improve our platform.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

Anthropic (Claude AI): Processes your inputs to generate analysis and content. Subject to Anthropic's usage policies.

Google: Authentication (Google Sign-In) and analytics (Google Analytics).

Stripe: Processes payment transactions securely.

Railway: Hosts our application infrastructure.

Cloudflare: Provides DNS and security services.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account information as long as your account is active. Service data (scorecards, reports, ad copy) is retained for 90 days after generation. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

5. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and Google Analytics cookies for understanding site usage. Google Analytics cookies are anonymized and do not personally identify you.

6. Data Security

We use HTTPS encryption for all data transmission, secure authentication via Google Sign-In, and encrypted storage for sensitive data. No system is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access your personal data, request correction of inaccurate data, request deletion of your data, withdraw consent for data processing, and export your data in a portable format.

These include the right to know what personal information we collect and how it is used, the right to request deletion of your personal information, the right to opt out of the sale of your personal information (we do not sell personal information), and the right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@soaraiusa.com.
