Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using our keyword research tool.

How is this different from paid keyword tools?

Our tool uses AI to provide contextual keyword recommendations tailored to your specific niche and competitive landscape — not just raw data dumps. The free version covers most needs; our Pro tier adds deeper competitor analysis and content briefs.

Can I research keywords for local SEO?

Yes. Specify your city, state, or region and the tool generates geo-modified keyword variations along with local search volume estimates, helping you target the exact areas where your customers are.

What is a keyword gap analysis?

A keyword gap analysis compares the keywords your competitors rank for against your own rankings. It reveals terms where competitors have visibility but you do not — representing the fastest path to capturing traffic you are currently missing.

How often should I do keyword research?

At minimum, quarterly. Search trends shift, new competitors enter your market, and seasonal demand fluctuates. Regular keyword research ensures your content calendar stays aligned with actual search behavior.

Do you support international keyword research?

Our tool currently focuses on English-language keywords with US, UK, Canadian, and Australian search volume data. Multi-language support is on our roadmap.
