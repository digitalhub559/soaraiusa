What Gets Scored in Your Landing Page Audit

Our AI analyzes 10 critical conversion factors that determine whether your landing page converts visitors into customers.

📝

Headline Effectiveness

Does your headline immediately communicate value and stop the scroll? We score clarity, urgency, and persuasion power.

💬

Value Proposition

Is it crystal clear what problem you solve and why someone should care? We evaluate specificity and resonance.

⭐

Social Proof

Do you build trust with reviews, testimonials, or case studies? Missing social proof kills conversions instantly.

🎯

CTA Design

Is your call-to-action button compelling, visible, and action-oriented? Button design directly impacts click-through rates.

🎨

Visual Hierarchy

Can visitors instantly understand what's most important? Poor visual hierarchy confuses users and drops conversions.

📱

Mobile Optimization

Does your page look perfect and convert on phones? Over 70% of traffic is mobile—this is non-negotiable.

⚡

Page Speed

Does your page load fast enough to keep visitors engaged? Every second of delay costs you conversions.

🔒

Trust Signals

Do you display security badges, certifications, guarantees? Trust signals directly influence purchase decisions.

✍️

Content Quality

Is your copy compelling, benefit-focused, and free of jargon? Poor copywriting is a silent conversion killer.

📋

Form Optimization

Is your form friction-optimized? Long, complex forms destroy conversion rates. We score field count, labels, and design.
